Brno Cinema Profiles, 1951-1953
Structure of cinema exhibition profiles in Brno analysed by country of production (1951-1953). The repeating pattern shows a predominance of Czech and Soviet films in the majority of cinemas. For an interactive graph, see: https://public.flourish.studio/visualisation/16931484/ .
Gent Cinema Profiles, 1951-1953
Structure of cinema exhibition profiles in Ghent analysed by country of production (1951-1953). The repeating pattern shows a predominance of Hollywood films in the majority of cinemas. For an interactive graph, see: https://public.flourish.studio/visualisation/16931958/
